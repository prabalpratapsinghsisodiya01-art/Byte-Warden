Our Security Architecture

Designed and built with absolute paranoia in mind.

Byte Warden uses standard, audited encryption algorithms to protect your data. Everything you save in your vault is encrypted before it ever leaves your device.

🛡️ AES-256 Encryption

Your vault data is encrypted using AES CBC 256-bit encryption. The U.S. government uses this exact same standard to protect Top Secret information.

🔑 PBKDF2 SHA-256

We use PBKDF2 SHA-256 to hash your master password and derive an encryption key. The high iteration count makes brute-force attacks computationally unfeasible.

☁️ End-to-End Encryption

Your data is encrypted locally on your browser. Any data synced remains in ciphertext. Even if our servers are compromised, the attackers get nothing but mathematical noise.

🔍 Open Source Ready

We believe in "trust through verification." By making elements of our application easily reviewable, security researchers around the world can verify our claims.
