Uncompromising Security Architecture

Byte Warden isn't just another password manager. Built from the ground up prioritizing a zero-knowledge architecture, your master password never leaves your device. All encryption and decryption is handled locally.

  • Military-Grade Encryption: PBKDF2 SHA-256 for key derivation and AES-256 bit encryption for your vault data.
  • Open Source Transparency: Verifiable code means security flaws cannot hide in obscurity.
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Seamlessly securely syncs across all your devices using encrypted blobs.
