Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Byte Warden, your privacy is our top priority. We employ a zero-knowledge architecture, meaning we cannot see, access, or sell your passwords or secure notes. We only collect the bare minimum necessary to provide you with the service, such as your email address for account creation.

2. How We Use Information

We use your email solely to authenticate you, communicate important account updates, and provide customer support. Your vault data is encrypted locally on your device before it ever reaches our servers.

3. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Your master password is never sent to our servers. It is used to generate an encryption key locally on your device, which is then used to encrypt your vault data. Because we never have your master password or encryption key, we cannot decrypt your vault data under any circumstances.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We may use third-party infrastructure providers to host our encrypted data and send transactional emails, but these providers are strictly vetted and do not have access to your unencrypted data.
